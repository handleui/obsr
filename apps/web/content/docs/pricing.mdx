## How It Works

Detent charges per successful resolve. No seat licenses, no monthly minimums.

## Credit Pricing

| Credits | Price | Per Credit |
|---------|-------|------------|
| 100 | $20 | $0.20 |
| 500 | $75 | $0.15 |
| 2,000 | $200 | $0.10 |

Credits don't expire.

## What Costs Credits

| Action | Credits |
|--------|---------|
| Autofix (formatting, imports) | Free |
| AI Resolve (simple fix) | 1 |
| AI Resolve (complex fix) | 2-5 |

Autofix handles deterministic fixes like formatting and import sorting. No AI, no cost.

AI resolves use Claude to understand and fix your code. Cost scales with complexity — a one-liner costs less than a multi-file fix.

## Free Tier

New accounts get **50 free credits**. Enough for ~30-50 resolves, depending on complexity.

No credit card required to start.

## FAQ

**What if a resolve fails?**
You're only charged for successful resolves.

**Can I set spending limits?**
Yes. Set per-project monthly budgets in the [Detent dashboard](https://detent.sh).

**Do credits expire?**
No.

**Can I get a refund?**
Contact [support@detent.sh](mailto:support@detent.sh) within 30 days of purchase.
